# Doctolib Product Strategy & Healthcare Customer Experience (CX) Architecture

Welcome to the Product Strategy and Customer Experience division of my portfolio. This repository hosts a comprehensive strategic optimization audit of **Doctolib**, Europe’s premier e-health scheduling and teleconsultation platform. This case study designs user experience improvements, builds stakeholder engagement loops, and sets up scalable measurement frameworks to boost user acquisition and long-term user retention.

---

## Case Study Executive Summary
**File:** `Doctolib.pdf` (Product Strategy & CX Research Deck)

In digital healthcare networks, product success depends on satisfying a highly fragmented ecosystem of stakeholders while reducing friction during onboarding. This project applies strategic product frameworks to segment users, map complex operational touchpoints, and engineer automated conversion loops tailored for distinct healthcare patient archetypes.

### Strategic Product Frameworks Applied

* **Stakeholder Relationship Topology:** Mapped the direct and regulatory value exchanges across the health ecosystem, detailing dependencies between patients, medical providers, cloud tech infrastructure, capital investors, and government regulators (CNIL / Ministry of Health).
* **Kano Feature Prioritization Matrix:** Classified system attributes to optimize engineering and product development roadmaps:
  * **Must-Be Requirements:** High-availability scheduling, proximity-based search filters, real-time booking updates, and zero-latency teleconsultation infrastructure.
  * **Performance Features:** UI rendering speeds, highly descriptive doctor profile cards, and deep algorithmic filtering options.
  * **Attractive Delighters:** Automated pre-filled medical paperwork, proactive preventative health nudges, and interactive post-visit summary timelines.
* **Dual-Segment Target Strategies (ICP):** Isolated distinct customer profiles to establish custom product growth initiatives:
  * **Segment A (Frequent Bookers):** Driven to increase repeat metrics via automated preventative routine reminders, early access to new feature releases, and immediate shortcut tools.
  * **Segment B (First-Time Patients):** Driven to minimize onboarding friction via targeted introductory user tours, interactive doctor-matching wizards, and 3-step lifecycle email flows.
* **Cross-Functional CX Measurement Architecture:** Designed an end-to-end telemetry system running on precise evaluation metrics:
  * **Hot Feedback Loops:** Prompt post-visit assessment triggers sent 2 hours after appointments to generate high-value, authentic provider reviews.
  * **Post-Booking Micro-Surveys:** Native micro-surveys measuring immediate digital booking satisfaction without disrupting the natural user flow.

### Key Competencies Demonstrated
* Product Management & Feature Prioritization (Kano Model Analysis)
* Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Segmentation & Behavioral Personalization
* Ecosystem Mapping & Multi-Stakeholder Relationship Engineering
* Customer Experience Architecture, Survey Design, & Operational Metric Tracking
